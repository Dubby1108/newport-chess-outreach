Scholarship Application

Cost should never be the reason a child misses out on chess. If a registration fee is a barrier for your family, please apply below — every application is reviewed individually and confidentially by our scholarship committee.

Your responses are confidential and are reviewed only by the Newport Chess Outreach scholarship committee for the purpose of awarding aid. The demographic questions further down (race/ethnicity, home language, and immigration status) are entirely optional and are never used to decide who receives a scholarship — we ask only to help us report our community impact to the funders who make this fund possible.
