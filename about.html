About Us

Built around a folding table and six chess sets

What started as a once-a-week library meetup is now a Newport County–wide nonprofit. Here's who we are and what we stand for.

Mission Statement

Spreading the opportunity and passion for chess within Washington

Newport Chess Outreach exists to expand access to chess education regardless of zip code, school budget, or family income. We teach chess not as an end in itself, but as a vehicle for the patience, planning, and quiet self-confidence that carry far beyond the board — into the classroom, the family, and the community. Our Story

From one library table to county-wide programs

Newport Chess Outreach began in 2019 with a folding table, six donated chess sets, and a single Thursday afternoon session at the Newport Public Library. What started as a way to give a handful of local kids somewhere to play after school grew, season by season, into weekly clubs at three libraries, a full slate of summer camps, and an annual tournament series drawing competitors from across the state.
